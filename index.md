## About Me
I am Jiahang Tu, a second-year Ph.D. student (2024-) in the College of Computer Science at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. [Hui Qian](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=n4csXw0AAAAJ). Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. degree (2020-2024) from the Chien-Shiung Wu College at Southeast University. My research centers on generative models and world models. In my earlier work, I focused on the safety and controllability of generative models, particularly diffusion-based methods for preventing the generation of unsafe or restricted content while preserving generative performance. More recently, my research has shifted toward unified multimodal models and world action models, where I study how perception, generation, and action can be integrated to support robotic understanding, embodied interaction, and predictive decision-making in dynamic environments.
